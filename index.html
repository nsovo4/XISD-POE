Who are we?

Acornhoek Church of the Nazarene is a Christian
congregation serving the Arconhoek community
and encouraging others through a life-changing
Christian journey. We seek to serve God by working
for justice, peace, respect and learning from all the
great faith traditions and desire to be known by
the love we have for one another.

Our Mission

We believe that education coupled with
evangelism, compassion, working for justice and
bearing witness to the kingdom of God are all
essential aspects of the mission to make disciples in
the nations.
